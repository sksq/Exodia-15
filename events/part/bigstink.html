Big Stink

The Street Play

organized by Samriddhi Jain — 9816923561

and Paawan Mukker — 9816923840

Let us a create a small world inside this world which will reflect this world the same way as a drop of water reflects the landscape.

Come with the social issues you want to eradicate from this society. It is to introduce more students to the raw and pedestrian, by socially relevant and effective form of theatre, street play or ‘nukkad naatak’. A street play is very different from mainstream theatre, mainly because it is performed outdoors, and not on a stage. A street play usually targets a crowd in a public place; the actors perform with audience on all sides. It takes up socially relevant subjects, mainly the issues that plague the society.

One entry per college.
1) Time limit is 20-25 minutes. Negative points for exceeding the time limit.
2) 10-15 members per team are allowed.
3) Use of mike and make up is strictly not allowed.
4) No props will be provided. Teams may use props but need to inform the event coordinator beforehand.
5) Decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Prizes worth Rs 15,000 to be won.
